Privacy Policy

This app is commited to using your Google information for the sole purpose of authenticating you. This allows you to prove who you are, and then access your private boats. We will store your Name and ID solely for the purpose of identifying you when using this API. Once you post your first recipe, your name will be publicly available as a user of the app. You can delete your account and all your recipes at any time by sending a delete request to "/users/<your ID>". Note that you will need to include your JWT for this delete request.
