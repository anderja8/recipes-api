Privacy Policy

This app is commited to using your Google information for the sole purpose of authenticating you. This allows you to prove who you are, and then access your private ingredients and recipes. We will store your Name and ID for the purposes of identifying you when using this API and displaying you in the list of active users found at the /users endpoint.\ \ Once you post your first recipe, your name will be publicly available as a user of the app. You can delete your account and all your recipes and ingredients at any time by sending a delete request to "/users/<your ID>". Note that you will need to include your JWT for this delete request to process.
